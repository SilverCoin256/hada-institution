FIG. AEFFECT-DIRECTION SCHEMATIC — MAGNITUDES IN THE PAPER
Measurement validity
Active
Four literatures quietly treat a proxy as the thing it measures. Recomputing one common SEC-oversight proxy against the SEC's own reporting-issuer counts, it reversed sign: the proxy's "active filer" denominator rose 186% over a period in which the SEC's own reporting-issuer count fell 20%. That result killed my original "regulatory-debt" thesis; the paper is now about the validation method, not the thesis. Proxy-validation work → · how the thesis died →

Authorization saturation
Working paper
A coupled two-queue model of the point where institutions swap slow substantive review for fast sign-off. In simulation, doubling reviewer capacity or halving AI throughput returns quality to baseline; raising the quality floor of fast sign-off reduces the damage but never removes the tipping point.
